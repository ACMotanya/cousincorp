Marketing Assistant

Cousin Corporation of America is currently looking to add a Marketing Assistant to our growing team! You will be assisting the Creative Director in ideating and developing strategies for various projects. That passion comes through as she/he engages with our internal teams and customers daily, with the goal of:

Essential Duties
  • Undertake daily administrative tasks to ensure the functionality and coordination of the department’s activities.
  • Support creative team in organizing various content projects and product development.
  • Provide insights based on trend data relative to new product launches and sales presentations.
  • Manage company’s marketing funnel including but not limited to our social media presence, website content, email marketing activities and digital advertising channels.
  • Assist in creating and managing an editorial calendar to ensure content is timely, relevant, and engaging.
  • Compose and post online content on the company’s website and social media accounts.
  • Conducting research to analyze competitive landscape, market trends and customer behavior and preparing reports by collecting, summarizing, and analyzing data to continuously improve traffic and capture more followers.
Desired Qualifications
  • Understands, or has the interest and ability to learn, emerging platforms, digital media, and web/social media management and measurement tools like Google Analytics.
  • Exquisite communication and people skills.
  • Good understanding of office management and marketing principles.
  • Demonstrable ability to multi-task and adhere to deadlines.
  • Well-organized with a customer-oriented approach.
  • Good knowledge of market research techniques and databases.
Minimum Requirements
  • High School diploma; BSc in Marketing, Business or relevant field is a strong plus.
  • Experience with Adobe Creative Suite and Microsoft Office products.
  • Being comfortable on camera and an interest in crafting a bonus!
Salary & Benefits
  • Competitive salary
  • Health, Dental, Vision, Life, Disability, 401K, paid vacation, and holiday pay
