Social Media Coordinator

Cousin Corporation is seeking two social media coordinators to help promote our brands! Cousin has two divisions – Cousin DIY and Laura Janelle – and we need your help to tell the world all about them! The ideal candidate has a great attitude, is extremely creative, has impeccable writing skills and loves, loves, loves research. The social media coordinators will become fully immersed in our brands and culture in order to be the voice of our company.

Responsibilities
  • Learning the DIY and gift industry – We are going to pay you to learn about two exciting industries!
  • Organizing, running, and growing Cousin’s day to day social media presence – We have lots of fans. We are looking for someone to tell us how to get even more!
  • Creating and posting exciting and engaging content
  • Designing and implementing social promotions and campaigns
  • Monitoring and responding to questions, feedback and online reviews to maintain an excellent reputation
  • Measuring, analyzing and reporting on reach and engagement on all social media channels
  • Improving SEO
  • Copywriting for thousands of products – At Cousin, we are constantly creating. Our product development team develops numerous product lines throughout the year, so you will have plenty of new products to promote!
  • Working with our Sales team to provide and post social media and marketing content for key accounts
Desired Skills
  • In-depth knowledge of various social media platforms including Facebook, Pinterest, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, etc. Please provide links as examples of previous experience.
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office products
  • Excellent creative and professional writing skills
  • Exceptional time management, organizational and prioritization skills
  • Capable of providing outstanding customer service on social media channels
  • Self-starter with the drive to continuously research, learn, and propose new strategies
  • Can demonstrate knowledge of SEO principles such as keyword research and Google Analytics.
  • Enjoyment of both the creative and analytical side of social media with the ability to present new ideas backed by research and analytical data
  • Ability to effectively communicate with senior level management – This position reports directly to the General Manager
  • Experience with Photoshop or other graphic design programs is a plus
  • Ability to maintain a high level of energy and positive attitude
Education/Experience
  • Minimum 2 years of experience in social media or marketing
  • Bachelor’s Degree
Salary and Benefits
  • Competitive salary and bonus
  • Health, Dental, Vision, Life, Disability, 401K, paid vacation and holidays

Purchasing Analyst

We are seeking a highly organized, self-motivated, detail-oriented Purchasing Analyst to join our growing team. The purchasing analyst collaborates with internal and external departments to maintain an uninterrupted supply chain. This position ensures that Cousin Corporation is able to meet our customers’ expectations with high quality product and high fill rates.

Responsibilities
  • Analyzes historical data and utilizes forecasts to monitor and replenish inventory levels in order to maintain an uninterrupted supply chain and high fill rate for major customers
  • Creates customized reports and analyzes large amounts of data using Microsoft Excel
  • Proactively coordinates with internal teams, freight brokers, and vendors to ensure that products are received correctly and on time
  • Enters, confirms, and receives purchase orders sent to international and domestic suppliers
  • Works closely with sales and design teams to accurately forecast future product demand
  • Collaborates with the Quality Control department to ensure that product exceeds customer expectations
  • Additional projects will be assigned regularly requiring the ability to work on your own or as part of a team to fulfill the requirements of the assignment
Minimum Requirements
  • Advanced Microsoft Excel skills
  • Strong working knowledge with Microsoft Office Suite (Outlook, Excel, Word, and Access is a plus)
  • Strong analytic and math skills
  • Ability to see trends and outliers in analyzing buying behaviors
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Ability to work well with repetition and fluctuating workloads
  • Possess initiative, prioritization, and multi-tasking skills to successfully manage multiple projects in a deadline-driven environment
  • Strong sense of urgency and responsibility in meeting deadlines and attaining goals
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment as well as individually
Desired Skills
  • Supply chain logistics experience is a plus
  • College Degree (Business, Mathematics, or related field) or equivalent work experience

This full-time position is Monday through Friday 8:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. We offer a very comprehensive benefit package.
