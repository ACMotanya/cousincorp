MERCHANDISING ASSISTANT

At Cousin, our team is energetic, dedicated, and passionate. Every day we strive to make a difference in the lives of our employees, our customers, and our suppliers. To support our continued growth we are hiring a Merchandising Assistant. The successful candidate will be a great person first, and a great business person second. This is an excellent opportunity to join our merchandising team in a support role.

Responsibilities
  • Independently perform product information assignments such as collecting, organizing, preparing and uploading product information
  • Process, format and revise data from various internal and external sources
  • Data Integrity: Work with Merchandising Manager in ensuring the accuracy of all product data in the system including product descriptions, required key data fields and images
  • Serve as subject-matter expert for all products sold to key accounts
  • Up to 10% travel may be required
Desired Skills
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office products
  • Advanced Excel skills, including pivot tables, lookups and other formulas
  • Competent in retail mathematics
  • Clear, concise written communication
  • Excellent time management and prioritization skills
  • Experience with visual merchandising and retail store planograms
Education / Experience
  • Minimum 2 years of experience in data management, sales, or customer service; business-to-business experience a plus
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Sales, Business Administration, Merchandising, or related fields
  • Two or more years of product information or catalog management experience
Salary and Benefits
  • Competitive salary and bonus
  • Health, Dental, Vision, Life, Disability, 401K, paid vacation and holidays

PURCHASING ANALYST

We are seeking a highly organized, self-motivated, detail-oriented Purchasing Analyst to join our growing team. The purchasing analyst collaborates with internal and external departments to maintain an uninterrupted supply chain. This position ensures that Cousin Corporation is able to meet our customers’ expectations with high quality product and high fill rates.

Responsibilities
  • Analyzes historical data and utilizes forecasts to monitor and replenish inventory levels in order to maintain an uninterrupted supply chain and high fill rate for major customers
  • Creates customized reports and analyzes large amounts of data using Microsoft Excel
  • Proactively coordinates and tracks shipments with internal and external departments to ensure that products are received correctly and on time
  • Enters, confirms, and receives purchase orders sent to international and domestic suppliers
  • Works closely with sales and design teams to accurately forecast future product demand
  • Collaborates with the Quality Control department to ensure that product exceeds customer expectations
  • Additional projects will be assigned regularly requiring the ability to work on your own or as part of a team to fulfill the requirements of the assignment.
Minimum Requirements
  • Advanced Microsoft Excel skills
  • Strong working knowledge with Microsoft Office Suite (Outlook, Excel, Word, and Access is a plus)
  • Strong analytic and math skills
  • Ability to see trends and outliers in analyzing buying behaviors
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Ability to work well with repetition and fluctuating workloads
  • Possess initiative, prioritization, and multi-tasking skills to successfully manage multiple projects in a deadline-driven environment
  • Strong sense of urgency and responsibility in meeting deadlines and attaining goals
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment as well as individually
Desired Qualifications
  • Supply chain logistics experience is a plus
  • College Degree (Business, Mathematics, or related field) or equivalent work experience
   

This full-time position is Monday through Friday 8:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. with a 30-minute lunch break. We offer a very comprehensive benefit package that includes medical, dental, life, short-term disability, vacation, holiday, personal time, 401(k) plan, an employee discount, and an end-of-year bonus plan.

SALES COORDINATOR

At Cousin, our key accounts are managed by a team of high quality, creative, and analytical professionals. Each team consists of a Sales Coordinator, designers, a Merchandiser and an Account Executive. This approach provides our retail partners with the extremely high level of attention and service they deserve. To support our continued growth, we are hiring a Sales Coordinator. A successful Sales Coordinator has a great attitude and always looks at the glass as being half full. This position is for someone who truly enjoys the challenge of in-depth data gathering and analysis. This is an excellent opportunity to join our sales team in a support role.

Responsibilities
  • Partner with Account Executive to provide best-in-class service to key account customers
  • Update and maintain product information in customer portals
  • Update weekly sales tracking files with customer Point-of-Sale data
  • Monitor weekly replenishment orders and highlight unusual order patterns
  • Partner with purchasing team to plan and track customer rollout and reset orders
  • Create detailed sales reports to thoroughly analyze all products in customers’ programs
  • Assist in creating, revising, copying and assembling presentations and sales call materials as needed by the sales team and work directly with Account Executive to prepare presentations, samples, and proposals
  • Serve as subject-matter expert for all products sold to key accounts
  • Up to 10% travel may be required
Desired Skills
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office products, especially Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint
  • Aptitude for analyzing, interpreting data, and communicating results
  • Competent in retail mathematics
  • Clear, concise written communication
  • Excellent time management and prioritization skills
  • Experience with visual merchandising and retail store planograms
Education / Experience
  • Minimum 2 years of experience in sales or customer service; business-to-business experience a plus
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Sales, Business Administration, Merchandising, or related fields
Salary and Benefits
  • Competitive salary and bonus
  • Health, Dental, Vision, Life, Disability, 401K, paid vacation and holidays
