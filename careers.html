Graphic Arts Coordinator

Cousin Corporation is seeking a Graphic Arts Coordinator to add to our growing team! Cousin has two divisions – Cousin DIY and Laura Janelle. The Graphic Arts Coordinator’s primary role is to act as a liaison between our Graphic Arts team and our Product Development team to ensure graphic related projects are completed in a timely manner for both divisions. The ideal candidate has a great attitude, is extremely creative, enjoys handling multiple projects and thrives in an extremely fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities
  • Working closely with Graphic Designers and Product Development, as well as external resources and factories, to manage and traffic graphic related projects from concept phase through final delivery
  • Attending internal meetings such as key account meetings, product development meetings, and production meetings that need graphic design department representation to ensure that projects are being completed in accordance with appropriate timelines
  • Working with the Design Manager to assess new projects and assign them to the appropriate graphic artist
  • Managing a master project list that will give an accurate snapshot of project progress for DIY and Laura Janelle divisions
  • Communicating regularly with management, the graphic designers, the product development team, the sales team, other internal teams, and vendor partners to track and provide updates on project progress
  • Providing input, ideas, and concepts for the overall design direction for various graphic projects. Examples of graphic projects to be managed include, but are not limited to:
    • • Developing attractive, on trend packaging header art, displays, display signage, packaging methods, marketing materials, promotional items, advertisements, brochures, flyers and website graphics
    • • Developing detailed specs for packaging art, product displays and packing instructions for products for domestic and overseas vendor partners
    • • Obtaining and negotiating best price quotes for print jobs and displays for various projects
    • • Approving print proofs and artwork received from various vendors
    • • Organizing new catalog production through composition, final print and fulfillment
    • • Providing graphic support for sales presentations developed for major retail partner meetings
    • • Creating realistic mockup packaging, displays and program samples for all sales presentations and buyer requests
    • • Working closely with imaging team to ensure all photography needs are being met for both divisions
    • • Coordinating 3D imaging support for any and all new projects and concepts
    • • Creating exciting art and imaging for all major client .com marketing
    • • Generating interesting graphics, photo imaging, and videos for all internal and client social media requests
    • • Managing and organizing digital assets for future use
  • Handling general department needs, such as coordinating printer maintenance and ordering materials
Desired Skills
  • Extensive knowledge of graphic design techniques and print production
  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite and Microsoft Office products
  • High proficiency in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop and Microsoft Excel
  • Able to take initiative to complete tasks and projects
  • Must be able to work both independently and in a group environment
  • Must have an understanding of relevant advertising trends
  • Ability to manage multiple projects at one time, possessing excellent organizational, analytical and logistical planning skills
  • Ability to effectively communicate with creative teams, sales teams and senior level management
  • Ability to maintain a high level of energy and positive attitude, while meeting or beating all deadlines in a fast-moving environment
Education / Experience
  • Minimum two years of experience working in a creative processing environment
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Graphic Design or related field
Salary and Benefits
  • Competitive salary and bonus
  • Health, Dental, Vision, Life, Disability, 401K, paid vacation and holidays

Designer -- Finished Fashion Jewelry and DIY Programs

Support and drive the development process encompassing the full product development spectrum from concept to sample management, design aesthetic, quality, margin requirement, and time line.

Responsibilities
The successful individual will leverage their proficiency in Product Development to...

  • Execute day to day tasks based on priorities set by Design Manager and drives task completion.
  • Operate with working knowledge of the development process and how it relates to the Product Development Timelines (Concept, Design, Merchandising, Materials, Production, and Packaging)
  • Send development packages to overseas partners and sample facilities.
  • Initiate and keep track of status of material and sample requests, approvals, and alert management of exceptions.
  • Support the review of prototypes to meet Product Development Timeline milestones with a focus on product execution, consistency of details, and target margin.
  • Understands and upholds Designs aesthetic intent. Executes, updates and maintains necessary tools (Spec Sheets, Milestone Dates, and constant communication and documentation on development).
  • Responsible for daily communication with overseas vendors.
  • Builds effective relationships with internal partners in both PD teams, Merchandising, Graphic Arts and Production to help execute cross functional synergy and collaboration.
  • Demonstrate understanding of costing implications as they relate to the Product Development process.
  • Manage development costs, packaging costs, and final costs.
  • Review spec sheets and validate cost components/ factory charges against necessary tools (i.e. quality, consistency, and samples).
  • Update and maintain accurate costs in all internal costing tools based on product updates and costing milestones.
  • Communicate to merchandising team on any updates or corrections made.

  • Excellent communication skills (oral and written)

  • Must be able to perform efficiently in a high pressure, fast paced environment
